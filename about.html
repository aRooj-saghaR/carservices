EUROPRO'S

TEAM OF EXPERTS

SVEN

Qualifications:

  • Multiple VW Factory Certifications
  • ASE Certifications
  • AAS Degree from ITT Technical

The founder of EuroPro, Sven started as a diesel mechanic in Slovakia in 1984. Sven immigrated to the Seattle area in 1989 and was soon a VW technician at Carter VW of Seattle. Throughout the next 10 years, he continued his career at 3 other dealerships, improving his skills in VW, Audi and BMW repair.


Sven and his family relocated to Spokane in 1999, where Sven spent the next 12 years as a VW Technician and the shop foreman at a local VW/Audi dealer. Afterwards, in 2011, he opened EuroPro and, in the beginning, was the sole technician. Today, he oversees the shop with 4 technicians and works at the front desk as a service adviser and customer service. Consequently, Sven’s knowledge, experience, and training now spans 32 years of dedicated European automobile service and repair making him an expert in the field.


In his free time, he enjoys time with his wife, family and friends. Additionally, he works on custom car projects, hikes, and trains his German Shepherd, Thor.
