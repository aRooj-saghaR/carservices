How It Works ?

ServiceMyCar is easy and intuitive to use with a rapid booking process. You can make a booking for one of our services in under 5 minutes. Scroll down for a step-by-step guide on how our service works

Play Video

Step 1 – Book A Service

Choose your desired service on our app, or the website. Fill in all the required details and choose a collection time slot.

Step 2 – Collection

Our designated truck driver will collect your vehicle from your chosen location at your chosen time.

Step 3 – Report

A comprehensive health report will be carried out on your vehicle. This will be sent to you shortly, informing you of any issues found. Along with the report, a quotation for the service parts, and any additional repairs, will also be sent to you.

Step 4 – Labour

After receiving your approval, our team of auto service expert will get to work. Upon completing the service, and any additional repairs, a final quality check will be carried out on your vehicle.

Step 5 – Delivery

One of our designated drivers will handover your vehicle back to you at your chosen location. Here’s where you can sign off on the service, and leave us a review.
